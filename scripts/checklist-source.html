The Narrative Audit Checklist

10 questions to diagnose whether your story is costing you deals.
Answer honestly. Every gap is a revenue leak.

01
What does your company do, in one sentence, without jargon?
02
If your best customer described you to a friend, what would they say?
03
What makes you different from the next three competitors?
04
When did you last post on LinkedIn? What happened?
05
Have you ever lost a deal, a hire, or an investor because of low visibility?
06
What is your biggest frustration with how you show up online?
07
Have you worked with a ghostwriter, agency, or content person before? What went wrong?
08
What does good content look like to you? Share an example you admire.
09
If we start next week, who else needs to say yes?
10
What would make this investment obviously worth it in 90 days?
